Hi, I'm Annie. I'm a User Experience Designer

I am an experience designer with a passion for all things creative and interactive, and believe design is personal and human. I have an M.S. in Creative Technologies and Design, and a B.S. in Computer Science. I have a unique combination of design, research, and development expertise that enable me to tackle a diverse set of user experience projects.
